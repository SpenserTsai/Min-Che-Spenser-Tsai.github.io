## Research Overview

My research lies in the intersection of control engineering, system identification, and intelligent mechatronic systems.

## Research Areas

- Control Systems
- System Identification
- Filtering and State Estimation
- Optimization Algorithms
- Embedded Systems
- Digital Twin
- Intelligent Mechatronic Systems


## Research Topics

### 1. Control Systems
Development of advanced control strategies for nonlinear and uncertain mechanical systems, including robust and model-based control design.

### 2. System Identification
Parameter identification for dynamic systems with measurement noise, model uncertainty, and nonlinear behavior.

### 3. State Estimation
Filtering-based estimation methods such as EKF, UKF, and optimization-assisted estimation for unmeasurable states and parameters.

### 4. Digital Twin
Integration of physical models, sensing, estimation, and data-driven support for digital-twin-enabled monitoring and analysis.

### 5. Embedded Systems
Implementation of control and estimation algorithms on embedded platforms for real-time engineering applications.

### 6. Intelligent Mechatronic Systems
Applications to cooling fan systems, mobile robots, and other electromechanical platforms requiring accurate modeling and monitoring.
