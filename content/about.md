        <h2>Biography</h2>
        <p>
          Min-Che Tsai (Spenser Tsai) was born in Changhua, Taiwan, in 1995. He received the B.S. degree in mechanical and computer-aided engineering from Feng Chia University (FCU), Taichung, Taiwan, in 2018, and the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in aeronautics and astronautics from National Cheng Kung University (NCKU), Tainan, Taiwan, in 2021 and 2026, respectively.
        </p>
        <p>
          His research interests include system modeling and identification, intelligent control techniques, robust controller and observer design, system analysis, and their practical applications in robotics and intelligent manufacturing systems.
        </p>
